About

After the BuildSpace program, many of us realized that the journey doesn’t end there—it’s just the beginning. We understand the importance of staying consistent and pushing forward, even after the structured environment of a program ends. This initiative is all about creating a support system where like-minded entrepreneurs can continue to grow together. We meet weekly to share our wins, mistakes, fails, challenges, and progress, providing that extra push we all need to keep moving forward.
